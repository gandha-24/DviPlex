👋 1. Introduction

Welcome to DviPlex ("we," "us," or "our"). DviPlex is a portfolio and showcase website for personalized digital artwork. We display our products online, and customers are directed to external platforms to complete purchases.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information collected when you visit our website. By using DviPlex, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you disagree, please discontinue use of the website.

We are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with applicable laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where relevant.

📋 2. Information We Collect

DviPlex is primarily a display website. We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate and improve the site.

2.1 Basic Website Usage Data

When you visit DviPlex, our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) and analytics tools may automatically collect basic technical information, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or URL
  • Approximate geographic location (country or region level)
  • IP address (processed by third-party services, not stored by DviPlex)

This information is used solely for analytics and website improvement. It is not used to personally identify you.

2.2 Contact Enquiries

If you contact us via email at support@dviplex.com, we may collect your name and email address solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not retain this information in any database beyond the scope of your conversation.

2.3 Cookies and Analytics Data

DviPlex may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand how visitors interact with the site. Please see Section 6 – Cookies for full details.

⚙️ 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Website operation: To ensure the website functions correctly across devices and browsers.
  • Analytics: To understand visitor behaviour, measure traffic, and improve content and layout.
  • Communication: To respond to direct enquiries or messages you send us.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent or malicious activity.
  • Legal compliance: To meet any applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

📝 4. Custom Orders, Payment, and Digital Product Terms

Important Digital Delivery Policy: All items sold on DviPlex are 100% digital products. No physical posters, canvases, frames, or prints will be shipped. Handcrafted digital artwork is delivered directly to your email address within 5 hours of order confirmation and image upload.

When you place an order on our Order Page, we collect your name, phone number, email address, custom text requirements, and reference photo files. This information is processed solely to design, customize, and deliver your personalized portrait.

4.1 Payment Gateway (Razorpay)

Payments are securely processed via Razorpay. We do not store or process card numbers, banking details, or UPI credentials on our servers. Razorpay processes all transactions securely under strict PCI-DSS compliance guidelines.

4.2 Strict No-Return / No-Refund Policy

Due to the fully customized, handcrafted, and digital nature of personalized digital artwork:

  • No Refunds: All payments are final once the transaction is completed and artwork creation begins.
  • No Cancellations: Orders cannot be canceled or modified once your reference photos have been processed.
  • No Returns: Digital file downloads cannot be returned or swapped.
  • Free Revisions: We want you to love your art. We provide free design adjustments and revisions within 24 hours of delivery.

🔗 5. Third-Party Services

DviPlex integrates with or links to the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy, which governs any data they collect independently.

Pinterest
We may link to our Pinterest profile or feature content from Pinterest. If you interact with embedded pins or visit our Pinterest page, Pinterest may collect data per their Privacy Policy. DviPlex does not control Pinterest's data collection.
Google Analytics
We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymised usage statistics such as page views, session duration, and traffic sources. Google Analytics may use cookies for this purpose. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on. See Google's Privacy Policy.
Cloudflare Pages
DviPlex is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, which may collect server logs including IP addresses and access times for security and performance purposes. Review Cloudflare's Privacy Policy for full details.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with through or in connection with DviPlex.

🍪 6. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. DviPlex may use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function correctly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Used by tools such as Google Analytics to collect anonymised usage data. These help us understand how visitors use our site.
  • Third-party cookies: Set by embedded services such as Pinterest or Etsy widgets, where applicable.

Your choice: Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies via their settings. You can also opt out of Google Analytics cookies using the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect some website functionality.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have the right to withdraw consent for non-essential cookies at any time.

🛡️ 7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect any data associated with the operation of DviPlex. Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, which employs industry-standard security protocols including HTTPS encryption for all traffic.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of information transmitted to or from our website.

Because DviPlex does not store payment information or maintain personal customer accounts, the risk of sensitive data exposure on our platform is minimal by design.

🌐 8. External Links

DviPlex contains links to external websites, including Etsy, Pinterest, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and Gumroad. These links are provided for your convenience and do not signify endorsement of those websites or their content.

We have no control over the content or privacy practices of external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any external website you visit before providing personal information.

🧒 9. Children's Privacy

DviPlex is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@dviplex.com and we will promptly delete such information.

📝 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of DviPlex after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

✉️ 11. Contact Information

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, please reach out to us directly.
